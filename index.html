A little bit about me.

I’ve made my home base in Salt Lake City, Utah after going to University. My journey into tech started with a degree in marine biology. As I started to write about my marine journey, I became very interested in blogging and then discovered how fascinated I was with web design and user experience. I was hooked and wanted to learn more about coding.

That curiosity opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website I realized I found something that satisfied my analytical science brain and my creative, artsy brain. Now I’m currently taking more classes to move into front-development and user experience where I can use my organization skills and eye for beauty to design clean, elegant code.
